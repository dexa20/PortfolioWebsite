My Work

Quiz App for Programming Skills

As part of a team assignment for the "Computer Challenge" module at the University of Central Lancashire, we developed an interactive quiz app using MIT App Inventor to assess programming skills. Collaborating closely with team members, we collectively designed the user interface, implemented question logic, and evaluated user responses. The quiz app was designed to focus on programming concepts, offering an engaging self-assessment tool for individuals looking to gauge their programming knowledge and skills.

3D Car Game using TL Engine (C++)

For our project in the "Game Concepts" module at the University of Central Lancashire, we undertook the design and development of a 3D car game. To bring this game to life, we harnessed the power of the TL Engine, a game engine provided by UCLan, which enabled us to craft an immersive gaming experience. Throughout the development process, we showcased our problem-solving prowess by implementing intricate features, including collision detection and dynamic particle movement. This project not only enriched our understanding of game development but also demonstrated our ability to tackle complex challenges in the field.
